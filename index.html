Work

EduVent
EduVent

EduVent is an app that easily informs students on upcoming events, as well as help plan and visualize their schedule for these events.

Research such as a questionnaire was made to help us inform our design. From our research we created Empathy Maps. Journey Maps, and Personas to help us understand our users.

Finally we created prototypes in Figma based on our research, that we would eventually use in our usability tests where we utilized a "Think aloud protocol"

Nügu App
Nügu App

The focus of this app was not the design but rather the research instead. As part of our research methods class we were tasked with conducting many methods of qualitative research.

Our topic was mapping apps, so we organized observational studies, focus groups, interviews, usability testings and created surveys and questionnaires.

All of this research was to create an improved map app, so our final findings and recommendations were to optimize search engines, provide a better offline experience, and implement ways to send others user suggested locations.

GRBS Website Redesign
GRBS Website Redesign

This project had a heavy focus on Information Architecture. The website we were tasked to restructure and redesign was the Grand River Blues Society website.

Within this project we conducted a Content Inventory and audit to obtain knowledge of the website's content and where things live. Then we used a Open Card Sort to gain some understanding of a user's mental model when using the site, as well as what labels they would use for our content. Finally, we created Tree Tests to find out where users were getting lost, and get feedback on a revised struture to see if there were any improvements.

At the end of all our research we created a sitemap and wireframes to explain our design recommendations, as well as our recommended metadata and controlled vocabulary (since the website had none originally).
